📋 Spotting Tips

🕐 Best time: 30-90 minutes after sunset or before sunrise (satellites are sunlit but sky is dark).

👀 How to spot: Satellites look like slow-moving "stars" that don't blink. Aircraft blink.

🏗️ ISS: The brightest — brighter than any star, moves west to east in 3-5 minutes.

📶 Starlink: Look for a "train" of dots in a line, or individual faint moving points.
