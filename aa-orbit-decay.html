💀 The Starlink Storm (Feb 2022)

A geomagnetic storm increased atmospheric density at 210 km by 50%. 40 freshly-launched Starlink satellites couldn't raise their orbits fast enough. They burned up within days. Cost to SpaceX: ~$10 million. The Sun controls satellite lifetimes — not engineers.
