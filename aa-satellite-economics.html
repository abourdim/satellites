💸 Spectrum Auctions

The FCC has auctioned over $250 billion in radio spectrum since 1994. Satellite frequencies (C-band, Ka-band) sell for billions. Frequency is the most expensive real estate in space.

🛡️ Space Insurance

Insuring a $200M satellite costs $10-30M (5-15% of value). Launch failure? Total loss. About 1 in 20 launches fail. Premiums have dropped as rockets get more reliable.

📦 CubeSat Revolution

A 3U CubeSat costs $50K-$500K to build and $100K to launch. Universities and startups now access space for the price of a house. Democratizing orbit.

☽ Heritage

Arab traders used stars for navigation — enabling the spice trade worth billions in today's money. Al-Khwarizmi's algorithms underpin every GPS transaction. The economic value of the sky is ancient.
